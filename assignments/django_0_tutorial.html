Walk Through the Django Tutorial¶

The Django project website has a fantastic tutorial. It walks you through the process of creating your first basic Django app. You’ve all worked through the tutorial at one point or another, but after the first half of this class you will likely gain a bit more from it than you did before.

Tasks¶

Review the tutorial. Simply read through it without doing any of the steps unless you feel compelled to do so. Think about the ways in which the work outlined by the tutorial relates to the work you have done in Pyramid over the last weeks.
